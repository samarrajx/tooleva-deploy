Why Convert JPG to PNG?

JPGs are great for photos, but what happens when you need sharp lines, text overlays, or transparent backgrounds? That's where PNGs shine.

Transparency

The biggest reason to convert a JPG to a PNG is the need for alpha channels (transparency). While translating a flat JPG won't magically create transparency, converting it to a PNG is the necessary first step before using tools to remove the background.
