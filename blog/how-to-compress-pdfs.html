How to Compress PDFs for Free (Without Losing Quality)

Published on March 12, 2026

Oversized PDF files can be a nightmare to email and slow to load. Fortunately, compressing them is incredibly easy and entirely free when you use the right browser tools.

Why Do PDFs Get So Big?

PDFs typically balloon in size due to high-resolution embedded images, uncompressed fonts, and hidden metadata generated by software like Adobe Illustrator or Microsoft Word.

The Privacy Problem with Most Compressors

If you Google "Compress PDF," you'll find hundreds of services. The problem? Most require you to upload your sensitive tax documents, contracts, or assignments to their remote servers. This introduces a major privacy risk.
