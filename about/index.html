About Tooleva

Tooleva was founded in 2026 with a simple mission: to provide the world with powerful, free digital tools that respect user privacy.

Our Privacy-First Approach

Most online PDF compressors and image converters require you to upload your sensitive files to their remote servers. We fundamentally disagree with that model.

Every single tool on Tooleva runs entirely within your web browser (client-side processing). Once you load our website, your files never leave your device. We use modern WebAssembly and advanced browser APIs to crunch pixels and parse PDFs right on your local machine.

Why Free?

We believe essential utilities shouldn't be locked behind expensive subscriptions or aggressive paywalls. We keep the lights on through unobtrusive Google AdSense placements, ensuring the tools remain free for students, professionals, and users worldwide.
