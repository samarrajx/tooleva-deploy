Secure & Private PDF Compression

Most online PDF compressors require you to upload your sensitive contracts, tax documents, or assignments to a remote server. This is a massive privacy risk. Tooleva uses WebAssembly and advanced browser APIs to process your files completely on your device.

As soon as you select your file, your computer's RAM and CPU take over, optimizing the PDF objects and saving you space. Nothing is ever sent to our servers.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it completely free to compress PDFs?

Yes! Tooleva is 100% free to use. We do not place watermarks on your documents or limit your file sizes arbitrarily behind a paywall.

Will the quality of my PDF degrade?

Our tool focuses on removing metadata and optimizing the internal structure (streams) of the PDF without significantly altering the visual elements mapping. However, heavily image-based PDFs may see less compression if the images are already optimal.
